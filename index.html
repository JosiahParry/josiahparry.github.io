About me:

Here you can take a look into the things I do, write about, am passionate about and enjoy.

I am a student at Plymouth State University studying Anthropology and Sociology (my concentration is in sociology), minoring in mathematics, and a certificate in Geographic Information Systems (GIS). I use statistical modelling and map making as a way to further understand social problems. My workflow usually consists of R, QGIS, ArcGIS, and Git (just for those times that I completely ruin everything).

I'm originally from the San Francisco Bay Area and could not be prouder, yahemmi? But I currently reside in the beautiful White Mountains of New Hampshire. This extreme antithesis of world-views has greatly shaped my persepctive on modern socio-politico issues, thus leading me to persue a future degree in Public Policy to drive towards more "data driven" and objective policy that can be non-partisan.

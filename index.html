About me:

Here you can take a look into the things I do, write about, am passionate about, and enjoy.

I am a student at Plymouth State University studying Anthropology and Sociology (my concentration), minoring in mathematics, and pursuing a certificate in Geographic Information Systems (GIS). I use statistical modeling and map-making as a way to further grasp social problems. My workflow usually consists of R, QGIS, ArcGIS, and Git.

I'm originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, but I currently reside in the beautiful White Mountains of New Hampshire. This juxtaposition of world views has greatly shaped my perspective on modern socio-political issues, leading me to pursue a degree in sociology in an effort to create a more data driven and non-partisan policy.
